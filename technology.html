Our Architecture

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

All inbound and outbound data is encrypted at transport and application layers — preserving integrity at every hop.

🕶️ API Obfuscation & Metadata Masking

URLs, headers, metadata—everything is scrubbed and anonymized before it routes out of your network.

📡 Stateless Zero-Knowledge Relays

No request bodies or logs are stored. Relays act purely as transient middleware—then vanish.

🧠 On‑Premise LLM Hosting

Deploy models inside your own infrastructure using our hardened containers—guaranteed zero data leakage.
